Why Appwrite

Because Appwrite feels like it's built by people who actually care about how developers *feel* when they use a tool.

I like products that don't just ship features, but think about clarity, learning, and the long-term relationship with their users. Appwrite is open source, opinionated in a good way, and clearly trying to reduce complexity instead of adding to it. That matters to me.

I'm also drawn to teams where documentation, writing, and collaboration are treated as core work, not side quests. From the outside, Appwrite looks like a place where good docs and developer experience are part of the product itself. That's exactly the kind of thing I want to help build.
